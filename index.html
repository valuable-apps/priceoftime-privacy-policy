Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: June 2026

Price of Time ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for your personal data is:

Price of Time
Contact email: priceoftimesupport@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect

Account Data

When you register or sign in via Google or Facebook, we collect your name and email address. Legal basis: performance of contract (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)).

Financial Data

You voluntarily enter your salary, working hours, and monthly expenses. This data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced to Google Firebase Firestore to enable multi-device access. Legal basis: performance of contract and your consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b) and 6(1)(a)).

Purchase History

Items you scan or manually enter (price, category, work-time equivalent, decision) are stored locally and optionally synced to the cloud. Legal basis: performance of contract (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)).

Advertising Data (Free Tier)

The free version of the app displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process data including device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization). See Google's Privacy Policy for details. Premium subscribers do not see ads.

Payment Data

Premium subscriptions are processed exclusively by Google Play Billing. We do not collect or store your payment card details. See Google Payments Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the app's core functionality (converting prices to work-time equivalents)
  • To sync your data across your devices (if cloud sync is enabled)
  • To track your savings goals and display statistics
  • To send optional daily motivational notifications (only if enabled by you)
  • To serve advertisements in the free tier via Google AdMob
  • To process Premium subscription payments via Google Play Billing

We do not sell, share, or use your personal data for any purpose other than those listed above.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Authentication & Firestore (Google LLC) — user authentication and cloud data storage
  • Google Sign-In (Google LLC) — optional social login
  • Facebook Login (Meta Platforms, Inc.) — optional social login
  • Google AdMob (Google LLC) — advertising in the free tier
  • Google Play Billing (Google LLC) — Premium subscription payments
  • ML Kit (Google LLC) — on-device text recognition for scanning price tags (no data is sent to Google servers)

Each service operates under its own privacy policy. We are not responsible for third-party data practices.

5. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States and other countries where Google LLC and Meta Platforms, Inc. maintain servers.

These transfers are subject to appropriate safeguards as required by the GDPR, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission. For more information, see Google's GDPR commitments.

6. Data Retention

  • Account & financial data: retained for as long as your account is active
  • Purchase history: records older than 1 year are automatically deleted
  • After account deletion: all personal data is deleted within 30 days
  • Advertising data: governed by Google AdMob's own retention policies

7. Data Security

All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). Local data is stored in a private app database not accessible to other apps on your device. Cloud data is protected by Firebase security rules and Google infrastructure security.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, you have the following rights:

Right of Access Request a copy of your personal data we hold
Right to Rectification Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
Right to Erasure Delete your account via Settings → Delete account, or visit our Data Deletion page
Right to Restriction Request that we limit processing of your data
Right to Data Portability Contact us to receive a copy of your data in a portable format
Right to Object Object to processing based on legitimate interests
Right to Withdraw Consent Withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior processing
Right to Complain Lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority (DPA)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at priceoftimesupport@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EEA where required). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via an in-app notice. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact Us

Price of Time — Privacy Inquiries Email: priceoftimesupport@gmail.com
We aim to respond to all privacy-related requests within 30 days.

Price of Time
Электронная почта: priceoftimesupport@gmail.com

2. Данные, которые мы собираем

Данные аккаунта

При регистрации или входе через Google или Facebook мы получаем ваше имя и адрес электронной почты. Правовое основание: исполнение договора (GDPR ст. 6(1)(b)).

Финансовые данные

Вы добровольно вводите зарплату, рабочие часы и ежемесячные расходы. Эти данные хранятся локально на вашем устройстве и по желанию синхронизируются с Google Firebase Firestore. Правовое основание: исполнение договора и ваше согласие (GDPR ст. 6(1)(b) и 6(1)(a)).

История покупок

Отсканированные или введённые вами позиции (цена, категория, эквивалент рабочего времени, решение) хранятся локально и по желанию синхронизируются с облаком. Правовое основание: исполнение договора (GDPR ст. 6(1)(b)).

Рекламные данные (бесплатная версия)

В бесплатной версии приложения показывается реклама от Google AdMob. AdMob может собирать и обрабатывать идентификаторы устройства, IP-адрес и данные о взаимодействии с рекламой. Вы можете отказаться от персонализированной рекламы в настройках устройства (Настройки → Google → Реклама → Отказаться от персонализации рекламы). Подробнее — в Политике конфиденциальности Google. Пользователи Premium рекламу не видят.

Платёжные данные

Платежи за Premium-подписку обрабатываются исключительно через Google Play Billing. Мы не собираем и не храним данные ваших платёжных карт.

  • Для обеспечения основного функционала приложения (перевод цены в рабочее время)
  • Для синхронизации данных между устройствами (при включённой облачной синхронизации)
  • Для отслеживания целей экономии и отображения статистики
  • Для отправки необязательных мотивационных уведомлений (только если включено вами)
  • Для показа рекламы в бесплатной версии через Google AdMob
  • Для обработки платежей за Premium через Google Play Billing

Мы не продаём, не передаём и не используем ваши данные ни в каких иных целях.
